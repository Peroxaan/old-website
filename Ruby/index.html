About Ruby

Ruby is a news app built in SwiftUI that optimizes your news experience, makes it cleaner, and more fun to use.

Elegant Design

Ruby is easy to use, and only shows you what's important. Ruby gets rid of all of the bloat you may find in other news apps.

Easy to Use and Understand

Ruby has everything you need, at a glance. It's simple to use - and you can understand it instantly.

Customize your Experience

You can easily change your Region or Language, for an optimal news experience. You can also block news sources that you might dislike.

Even More

With Ruby, you can save a news article to read later - or quickly share an article with a friend. It's that easy. Ruby is optimized for your device, and it'll be a great experience that feels native.
