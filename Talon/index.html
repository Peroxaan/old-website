About Talon

Talon is a simple tool for users to use, manage, and work with Webhooks on Discord and Slack.

Simple Design

Talon gets rid of everything complex, and gives you a nice and easy to use Interface thats familiar to use.

Ease of Use

It's very easy to use, and allows a larger audience to work with Webhooks in a simple way, without requiring you to know how to code.
