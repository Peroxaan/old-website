Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12th, 2020

Here at Peroxaan Studios, we believe in protecting our users privacy. Therefore, all of our products are designed to collect little to no data about you, and anything that is collected is purely data that the user consents to provide. When we’re collecting data, you will know. We don’t collect anything without letting our users know.

In Talon for iOS, as of beta 16 - nothing is being collected from the users by Peroxaan. All data you input in the App is handled within the App, and we do not have access to any of that data - and it is not saved during use. No data is collected by third parties, either. This is subject to changed in the future, since we plan to allow you save certain information within the app.

In Electrocam for iOS, as of beta 3 - nothing is being collected from the users by Peroxaan, and nothing is being collected by any third parties. This more than likely will not change.

In Peroxaan Stickers for iOS, as of version 1.3 - nothing is being collected from the users by Peroxaan, and this will not be changing.

For all of our apps, there are links to external websites - such as Twitter and Reddit. We have no control over what data they may collect. For that information, please read the Twitter and Reddit privacy policies.

Also, for all apps - Apple gives us access to your Crash Reports and Feedback, if you choose to submit it. Your name attached to your Apple ID and your email attached to your Apple ID may be shared if you submit a crash or send feedback. We also have access to the amount of sessions you’ve had in any given App.
